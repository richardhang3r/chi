Chi: Transform Screen Time into Active Time

"Earn Your Screen Time"

Welcome to Chi, the revolutionary app designed for parents to help their kids develop healthy habits while managing their screen time. Chi uniquely links health metrics with app usage, requiring kids to complete parent-selected exercises, verified by the phone's camera, before accessing their favorite apps. Imagine walking into your child's room to see them doing squats or pushups, all to unlock social media. Chi leverages metrics like daylight exposure, sleep hours, and stand hours to ensure balanced well-being. Plus, with Apple ID family settings, kids can't delete or override Chi without parental permission. Empower your parenting and motivate your kids with Chi.
